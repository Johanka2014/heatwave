Surviving the Heat: Essential Heatwave Vocabulary

A Complete Lesson Plan for B1 English Learners

This lesson introduces essential vocabulary related to extreme heat events, with practical exercises to develop speaking, reading, listening and writing skills whilst expanding students' climate-related lexicon.

What is a Heatwave?

[cite_start]

A heatwave is a period of unusually hot weather that typically lasts several days or weeks. [cite: 7] [cite_start]During this time, temperatures are significantly higher than the average for that location and season. [cite: 8] [cite_start]This extreme weather phenomenon often leads to drought conditions and can cause serious health concerns such as sunstroke if proper precautions aren't taken. [cite: 9]

[cite_start]

High humidity can make a heatwave feel even more intense as it prevents sweat from evaporating efficiently from our skin. [cite: 20]
