What is a Heatwave?

[cite_start]

A heatwave is a period of unusually hot weather that typically lasts several days or weeks. [cite: 7] [cite_start]During this time, temperatures are significantly higher than the average for that location and season. [cite: 8]

[cite_start]

This extreme weather phenomenon often leads to drought conditions and can cause serious health concerns such as sunstroke if proper precautions aren't taken. [cite: 9]

[cite_start]

High humidity can make a heatwave feel even more intense as it prevents sweat from evaporating efficiently from our skin. [cite: 20]
